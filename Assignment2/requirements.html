Program Requirements and Sequence

Program Requirements

To complete the CTK program, students must meet the following requirements:

  • Complete all required core courses with a minimum grade of C.
  • Complete a total of 120 credit hours.
  • Maintain a minimum GPA of 2.5.
  • Complete a capstone project or internship.

Course Sequence

The suggested sequence for completing the CTK program is as follows:

Freshman Year

  • CTK 201 - Basic Skills In The Digital Arts
  • CTK 202 - Foundations: Audio Theory
  • CTK 203 - Foundations: Recording and Sampling
  • CTK 204 - Foundations: Music Concepts

Sophomore Year

  • CTK 240 - Sound Design I: Beginning Digital Audio
  • CTK 260 - Audio Production I
  • CTK 260 - Arts Technology Performance Studio

Junior Year

  • CTK 300 - Web Video
  • CTK 301 - Web Design and Development
  • CTK 302 - Computer Programming for Creatives
  • CTK 303 - Advanced Web Design and Development

Senior Year

  • CTK 320 - Programming Dynamic Media
  • CTK 340 - Sound Design 2: Advanced Digital Audio
  • CTK 345 - Sequencing And Digital Audio
  • CTK 351 - Game Design 1
  • CTK 354 - User Experience Design for Games
  • CTK 377 - Motion Graphics
